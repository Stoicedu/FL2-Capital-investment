Regular Expenses

  • Used up quickly (within a year)
  • Examples: Office supplies, rent, marketing
  • Goes on Income Statement

Capital Investments

  • Provide value for many years
  • Examples: Buildings, factories, equipment
  • Goes on Balance Sheet as Assets
